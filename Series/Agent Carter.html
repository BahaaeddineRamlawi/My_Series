Agent Carter (2015-2016)

Life changes for Peggy Carter after her boyfriend Steve Rogers is feared dead. Now she leads a dual life by doing administrative work as well as working as a S.H.I.E.L.D. agent under Howard Stark.

Creators: Christopher Markus and Stephen McFeely

Writers: Jack Kirby, Stan Lee, Christopher Markus, Stephen McFeely and others

Directors: Jennifer Getzinger, Metin Hüseyin, David Platt, Lawrence Trilling and others

No. of seasons: 2

No. of episodes: 18

Running time: 41–43 minutes

Genres: Action-adventure - Period drama - Spy-fi - Superhero
